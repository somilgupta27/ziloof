Our Story

Every Bottle Has a Beginning.
Ours Begins With a Question.

Why should something as essential as mustard oil leave you wondering what's really inside?

For generations, mustard oil has been a staple in Indian kitchens—bringing families together through food, tradition, and wellness. Yet today, the shelves are crowded with countless brands, flashy labels, and marketing claims. Somewhere along the way, purity became harder to find.

We believed there had to be a better way.

That belief became Ziloof.

✦

We went back to what mattered most.

No shortcuts.
No unnecessary processing.
No compromises.

Just carefully selected mustard seeds, pressed once, preserving the oil's natural aroma, bold flavor, and nutritional goodness.

Every drop is produced with one simple promise:

"If we wouldn't serve it to our own family, we won't bottle it for yours."

"Pure by nature. Honest by choice. Ziloof."
